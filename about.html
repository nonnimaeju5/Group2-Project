Who We Are?

Travel Spirit

We believe travel is an essential pillar to living a life filled with purpose and meaning. It enables you to venture beyond your small pocket of the world and find beauty in the unknown. Sometimes we miss out on these oppurtunities waiting on travel companions, not knowing where to start or fearing solitude in an unfamiliar enviroment.

Our Mission

The mission of WeTravel is to eliminate these factors by creating an avenue to connect with other travelers and carefully curating fulfilling itineraries that allows you to truly indulge in new cultures – making life long memories with your safety and convenience in mind.From hiking in Iceland and meditating in Bali, to go-karting on the streets of Tokyo or exploring the luxuries of Dubai, we have an array of packages suitable for any experience you may be searching for. Whether you are a seasoned traveler or you finally mustered up the courage to take that first step, the travel packages we design are all about building lasting friendships with like minded individuals and confidence to take a chance and live life to the fullest; even if you’re riding solo!

Ready for the adventure of your life?

Travel experiences are the best way know yourself and reconnect with others, and with nature and the world around us. WeTravel and got you covered.
